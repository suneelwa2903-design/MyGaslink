# MyGasLink — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-06-20
**Last updated:** 2026-06-20
**Operator:** GasLink Consulting Solutions, Hyderabad, India
**Contact:** privacy@mygaslink.com

This Privacy Policy describes how MyGasLink ("we", "us", "the app") collects,
uses, retains, shares, and deletes the personal information of users —
distributor staff, drivers, and customer end-users — of the MyGasLink LPG
distribution management platform. It is published in compliance with the
**India Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP)**, the **Apple App
Store Review Guidelines §5.1.1**, and the **Google Play Data Safety**
disclosure requirements.

---

## 1. Personal information we collect

| Category | Specific data | When collected |
|---|---|---|
| **Account identifiers** | Email address, password (hashed), full name, phone number, role (customer / driver / inventory / finance / distributor admin / super admin) | At account creation by an administrator; updated via Profile |
| **Customer profile** *(B2B customers only)* | Business name, GSTIN, billing + shipping address, primary contact name + phone, credit terms | Captured at customer onboarding by distributor staff |
| **Location** | Approximate (network) + precise (GPS) coordinates | **Drivers only**, during active delivery routes. Single-shot reads via `expo-location.getCurrentPositionAsync` — we do **not** stream or store continuous tracks. |
| **Operational records** | Orders placed, deliveries confirmed, invoices issued, payments collected, cylinder inventory movements | Generated by user actions in the app |
| **Device diagnostics** | App crash reports, breadcrumbs leading up to a crash, anonymized device model + OS version | Captured by Sentry (see §5) only on preview / production builds and only when an error occurs |
| **Authentication metadata** | Login timestamp, IP address, user-agent string | On every login attempt, written to `login_history` for security forensics. Retained 180 days. |

**We do NOT collect:** photos, videos, microphone audio, contacts, calendar,
SMS, call logs, financial account credentials, biometric data, advertising
identifiers, browsing history, or social-network data.

## 2. How we use your information

- **Service delivery** — authenticate you, route your orders to drivers, generate
  invoices, track collections, produce inventory and tax reports.
- **Legal compliance** — generate GST e-invoices (IRN / EWB) via the NIC tax
  portal, maintain Income Tax / GST audit trails, respond to lawful requests
  from Indian authorities.
- **Security** — detect and prevent unauthorized access, debug crashes, audit
  sensitive actions (e.g. GST cancellation, customer credit changes).

We do **not** use your personal information for marketing, advertising,
analytics resale, profiling, or third-party data brokerage.

---

## 3. Legal basis (DPDP 2023)

We process personal information on the following bases under DPDP §7:

- **Performance of service** — collecting and using your name, email, phone,
  address, and operational records is necessary to operate the LPG distribution
  workflow you signed up for.
- **Legal obligation** — Indian GST law (Section 36 of CGST Act 2017) and
  Income Tax law (Rule 6F of Income Tax Rules 1962) require us to retain
  invoices, payment records, and audit trails for **eight (8) years** from
  the end of the financial year to which they relate.
- **Consent** — for location collection on driver devices, we obtain explicit
  consent at the OS permission prompt; the DPDP-compliant consent checkbox
  on first login covers data processing per §6 of DPDP.

## 4. Data retention

| Data | Retention period | Anchor |
|---|---|---|
| Account profile (name, email, phone, address) | Until you request deletion + 30-day grace period (§7 below) | DPDP §12 — Right to Erasure |
| Invoices, orders, payments, ledger entries | **8 years** from the end of the financial year, even after account deletion (anonymized — linked to a random ID, not to you) | CGST Act Section 36 + Income Tax Rule 6F |
| Login history (IP, user-agent, timestamp) | 180 days | Internal forensics minimum |
| Sentry crash logs | 90 days (Sentry default) | Sentry retention policy |
| Location coordinates | Not stored beyond the lifetime of a single delivery confirmation event | Service necessity |
| Email logs | 1 year | Compliance + bounce diagnosis |

After 8 years, all anonymized records are permanently deleted.

## 5. Third parties we share data with

We share the minimum personal information necessary, only with the following
service providers:

| Provider | What is shared | Purpose | Location |
|---|---|---|---|
| **NIC GST e-invoice + e-way bill** (via WhiteBooks) | Invoice line items, customer GSTIN, customer name + address, your distributor GSTIN | Legal requirement under CGST Act for B2B invoices > ₹500 and goods movements > ₹50,000 | India |
| **Sentry** (sentry.io) | Crash logs, breadcrumbs (request URLs, app actions leading to the crash), anonymized user id (no PII) | Diagnostics for app stability | EU (Frankfurt) / US |
| **Razorpay** *(if subscription billing enabled)* | Distributor admin name, email, phone | Process the distributor's SaaS subscription payment to GasLink | India |
| **AWS** (Mumbai region — `ap-south-1`) | All data above, encrypted at rest | Hosting infrastructure for the API + database | India |

We do **not** share personal information with advertising networks, data
brokers, analytics resellers, or any party not listed above.

## 6. Security

- All client-server traffic over HTTPS only. The app rejects plain HTTP.
- Passwords stored as bcrypt hashes; never in plaintext.
- Mobile auth tokens stored in `expo-secure-store` (Keychain on iOS,
  Keystore on Android) — never in `AsyncStorage`.
- The database runs in a private AWS subnet; access only via SSH bastion.
- All sensitive actions (GST cancellation, customer credit changes,
  payment approval, account deletion) write an audit log row with the
  acting user's id + IP + timestamp.

## 7. Account deletion — Your right under DPDP §12

You may request deletion of your account and the personal information
associated with it at any time, from any device:

- **In-app (mobile):** Settings → Account → Delete Account. Follow the
  three-screen flow. Your request is submitted immediately; deletion
  completes within 30 days.
- **Email:** Send a request to privacy@mygaslink.com from your registered
  email. We will action it within the same 30-day window.

**What happens after you submit a request:**

1. You are signed out and your login is blocked from all app features
   except the deletion-cancel screen. The request appears in your account
   immediately.
2. You have **30 days** to cancel the request by logging in and tapping
   "Cancel Deletion Request." After 30 days, the request cannot be reversed.
3. After 30 days, your personal information — name, email, phone, address,
   contact details, location records — is permanently removed.
4. **Invoices, orders, payments, and other financial records are retained
   anonymously for 8 years** as required by Indian Income Tax and GST law
   (CGST Section 36; Income Tax Rule 6F). Anonymized records are linked to
   a random ID and are not associated with you in any way.
5. After 8 years, all anonymized records are permanently deleted.

**Exceptions** under which a deletion request may be refused (DPDP §12(2)):
- An active legal dispute involving your account.
- A pending regulatory investigation.
- Outstanding financial dues > ₹100 to the distributor (you will be asked
  to settle the balance first).

**Special restrictions:**
- If you are the only `distributor_admin` on a tenant, you must first add
  a second admin so the tenant retains an administrator.
- Super admin accounts cannot be self-deleted — contact another super
  admin to action the deletion.

## 8. Your other rights under DPDP 2023

- **Right of access (§11):** Request a copy of all personal information we
  hold about you. We will respond within 30 days.
- **Right to correction (§12):** Request correction of inaccurate or
  incomplete data through the in-app Profile screen or via support.
- **Right of grievance redressal (§13):** If you have a complaint about how
  we handle your data, contact privacy@mygaslink.com. If unresolved within
  30 days, you may escalate to the Data Protection Board of India.
